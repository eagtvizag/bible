About Us

Growing Faith Through Good Literature


Located in the heart of PM Palem, Visakhapatnam, Christian Book Room is dedicated to providing high-quality spiritual materials to believers and truth-seekers.

We specialize in a wide range of Christian literature including Holy Bibles, Bible Dictionaries, theological books, and Sunday school materials available in both Telugu and English languages.

Our mission is to equip the local community with the Word of God and enriching books that build strong faith and deep biblical understanding.
